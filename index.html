About Us


Sphere Education Institute is a nationally recognised provider of vocational education and training degrees in Australia and offer verity of courses in business, hospitality, cookery, accounting, marketing, management, tourism, entrepreneurship, project management and leadership.

Sphere is a registered Institute of Vocational Education and Training on the Privately Operated Registered Training Organisation (RTO: 45176) and all courses provided are accredited by the the Australian Skills Quality Authority (ASQA).

The legal entity registered to trade as Sphere Education Institute is Sphere Education Institute Pty Ltd (ABN: 93 614 124 784).
